Our story

TerraBloom Global was born from a simple idea: to create a direct connection between farmers and consumers, cutting out the middlemen and bringing fresh produce straight from the fields to your table. We started in Taveta, where local farmers grow vibrant crops that deserve to be seen and enjoyed across Kenya.

Our platform allows farmers to upload their harvest details, share photos of their produce, and set prices that reflect their hard work. Consumers can browse these listings, choose what they need, and connect with our logistics team to get their orders delivered fresh and fast.

At TerraBloom, we believe in transparency, fairness, and community. By connecting growers directly with buyers, we support local agriculture, empower farmers, and create a healthier food ecosystem for everyone.

Logistics

Our logistics section focuses on speed, accuracy, and the final mile. Drivers and coordinators connect each farm harvest with the right consumer destination.

Transport pricing

  • Small cargo van: KES 1,500 per 10 km
  • Medium truck: KES 4,500 per 20 km
  • Large flatbed truck: KES 8,000 per 30 km
  • Motorbike express delivery: KES 1,000 per 5 km

Routes and regions

We ferry goods across Kenyan towns, urban markets, and rural distribution hubs:

  • Taveta farms to Mombasa market
  • Nairobi fresh produce hub
  • Naivasha distribution center
  • Kilifi and coast delivery routes
